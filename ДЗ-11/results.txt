Pered Novym Godom v detskom sadu nariadili krasivuiu pushistuiu elochku. Deti sami veshali na nee raznye igrushki, shary, shishki, girliandy i flazhki. A malchik Sasha nadel na makushku elki krasnuiu zvezdu. Deti s neterpeniem zhdali Deda Moroza i druzhno krichali: «Ded Moroz!». Ded Moroz prishel k detishkam s bolshim meshkom podarkov. Podarkov khvatilo vsem.
